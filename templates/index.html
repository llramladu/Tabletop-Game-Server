About The Game Server

The Serpens team has completed a playable card game, similar to Uno.

In order to play you will need to have a user account on the server.

Once you have logged in you can start a game with other users, or play against our AIs.

The AIs are Computer_1, Computer_2 and Computer_3, please be nice to the AIs we don't want to start the next Skynet.
